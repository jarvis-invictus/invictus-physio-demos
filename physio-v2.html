Common Sports Injuries We Fix

From weekend warriors to professional athletes — we know exactly how to get you back on the field.

🦵

ACL & Knee Injuries

Pre and post-surgical ACL rehab, meniscus tears, patellar tendinitis, and knee pain from running or impact sports.

Avg. Recovery: 6–8 weeks
💪

Shoulder & Rotator Cuff

Swimmer's shoulder, rotator cuff tears, shoulder impingement, and throwing-sport injuries (cricket, badminton, baseball).

Avg. Recovery: 4–10 weeks
🦶

Ankle & Foot Injuries

Ankle sprains, Achilles tendinopathy, plantar fasciitis, and stress fractures common in runners and footballers.

Avg. Recovery: 3–8 weeks
🎾

Tennis & Golfer's Elbow

Lateral and medial epicondylitis — affecting racquet sports, cricket, and repetitive overhead athletes.

Avg. Recovery: 4–6 weeks
🏃

Runner's Injuries

IT band syndrome, shin splints, stress reactions, and biomechanical issues causing pain in distance runners.

Avg. Recovery: 3–6 weeks
🧠

Concussion & Sports Head

Return-to-sport protocol following concussion — cognitive and vestibular rehab for contact sport athletes.

Protocol: 7-stage RTS
Our Services

Evidence-Based Treatment Methods

We use the latest sports science and hands-on techniques to speed your recovery.

🔬

Sports Biomechanics Assessment

Full movement analysis to identify why you're injured — not just what hurts. Fix the root cause, prevent recurrence.

⚡

Dry Needling & IMS

Intramuscular stimulation for deep muscle knots and trigger points. Dramatically accelerates healing in 1–2 sessions.

🌊

Shockwave Therapy

Non-invasive high-energy waves for chronic tendon injuries (Achilles, patellar, plantar fascia). Faster than surgery.

💪

Strength & Conditioning Rehab

Return stronger than before. Sport-specific strength programming from Week 1 to full return-to-competition.

🏅

Performance Optimization

Injury prevention screening and performance tuning for athletes preparing for tournaments, marathons, or seasons.

🏠

Home Visit Physio

Post-surgical patients and elderly athletes get professional physio at home. Same quality, zero travel stress.
