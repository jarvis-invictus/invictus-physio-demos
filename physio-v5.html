FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to bring a prescription or referral?

Not required for general physiotherapy. For post-surgical rehab, please bring your operative notes or discharge summary. We accept walk-ins and online bookings.

How quickly will I see results?

Most patients feel meaningful improvement within 2–4 sessions for acute conditions. Chronic or post-surgical conditions typically require 8–16 sessions for full results. We'll set clear expectations at your first session.

Do you have female physiotherapists?

Yes. Dr. Nisha and Dr. Poonam are available for female patients who prefer a female therapist. Please mention this preference when booking.

Is your equipment hygienic and regularly serviced?

All equipment is sanitised between patients. Our electrotherapy and LASER equipment is calibrated and serviced quarterly by certified biomedical technicians.

Can you send reports to my doctor?

Yes, absolutely. We provide weekly progress summaries formatted for orthopaedic/neurology follow-up. Just share your doctor's email or WhatsApp and we'll coordinate directly.
