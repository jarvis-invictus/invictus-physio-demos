SECTION 3: TREATMENT TECHNOLOGY
🔬 Evidence-Based Methods

Treatment Technology
That Actually Works

No guesswork. Every technique we use is backed by peer-reviewed research and refined through years of treating competitive athletes.

🪡
Dry Needling
Precision trigger-point release that deactivates muscle knots and restores range of motion within minutes. Not acupuncture — this is neuroscience.
🩹
Kinesio Taping
Strategic elastic taping that supports muscles and joints without restricting movement. Play through recovery — the way pros do it.
📡
Ultrasound Therapy
Deep tissue heating via high-frequency sound waves. Accelerates cellular repair, reduces inflammation, and speeds up collagen synthesis.
📐
Biomechanical Analysis
Video-based movement screening to identify faulty patterns that cause recurring injury. We fix the root cause, not just the symptom.
🔄
Return-to-Sport Protocols
Phase-by-phase progression from rehab to full competition. Objective benchmarks, sport-specific drills, and clearance testing before you step back on the field.
