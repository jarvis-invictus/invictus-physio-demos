Our Services

Evidence-Based Treatment Methods

We use the latest clinical techniques to deliver lasting relief, not just temporary fixes.

🙌

Manual Therapy

Hands-on joint mobilisation, soft tissue manipulation, and myofascial release. Effective for acute and chronic musculoskeletal pain.

⚡

Electrotherapy (IFT, TENS, US)

Interferential therapy, TENS, and therapeutic ultrasound for pain relief and tissue healing acceleration.

🏋️

Therapeutic Exercise

Personalised strengthening and flexibility programmes. Builds resilience so injuries don't recur.

🧲

Dry Needling & Cupping

Trigger point dry needling and myofascial cupping for deep muscle tension release and faster recovery.
