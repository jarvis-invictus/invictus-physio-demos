Common Questions

What People Usually Ask Before Booking

Do I need a doctor's referral to come in?

No referral needed for most musculoskeletal conditions. Walk in or WhatsApp us directly. For post-surgical cases, please bring your operative notes and surgeon's clearance so we can plan the right protocol from session one.

How many sessions will I realistically need?

We'll give you an honest estimate at the initial assessment. Acute sports injuries: typically 4–8 sessions. Chronic conditions: 8–16 sessions. Post-surgical rehab: 12–20 sessions. We don't pad session counts — our Google reviews reflect that.

Are home visits available? Which areas?

Yes. We cover Kothrud, Bavdhan, Shivajinagar, Aundh, Baner, and Karve Nagar. Dr. Vikram handles most home visits personally with a fully equipped kit. WhatsApp us to check availability for your area.

Do you accept health insurance?

Many health insurance providers reimburse physiotherapy. We provide detailed clinical receipts and documentation formatted for TPA claims. Our front desk team will help you with the paperwork.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Any relevant X-rays, MRI reports, or discharge summaries. Comfortable clothing that allows access to the affected area. If you have a referral from a doctor, bring that too — but it's not required.
