Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Do I need a doctor's referral to book?

No referral needed for most musculoskeletal conditions. For post-surgical rehab, we request the operative notes and surgeon's clearance — we can coordinate this directly with your doctor.

How many sessions will I need?

Depends on your condition. Acute injuries typically resolve in 4–6 sessions. Chronic conditions or post-surgical rehab may require 8–20 sessions. We'll give you an honest estimate at assessment.

Is physiotherapy covered under insurance?

Yes, many health insurance plans cover physiotherapy. We provide detailed receipts and clinical notes formatted for TPA/insurance reimbursement. Ask our front desk for assistance.

Do you treat children and elderly patients?

Absolutely. We have paediatric physio for developmental delays and sports injuries, and geriatric rehab for fall prevention, joint replacement recovery, and balance disorders.

What's included in the home visit service?

Our therapist brings all necessary equipment: TENS/IFT machine, ultrasound therapy unit, resistance bands, and exercise tools. The session is identical to our clinic session — just in your home.
