Do you provide home visit physiotherapy?
+
Yes, absolutely! Home visits are one of our core services. Dr. Sunita and her team bring all necessary portable equipment to your home — including resistance bands, balance boards, TENS machines, and assessment tools. We cover all of Kothrud, Karve Nagar, Warje, Bavdhan, Pashan, Aundh, and most of Pune. Home sessions are 45–60 minutes, just like clinic sessions.
How long does recovery typically take?
+
It varies based on the condition and individual health. Knee replacement rehab typically shows major improvement in 8–12 weeks. Hip fracture recovery takes 10–16 weeks. Chronic conditions like arthritis require ongoing management, usually 2–3 sessions per week initially, tapering to once a week. We set realistic milestones and celebrate every small win with our patients.
Is physiotherapy safe for someone who is 75+ years old?
+
Completely safe. In fact, it's especially important for patients 75 and above. Dr. Sunita specializes in geriatric rehab and designs every exercise to match the patient's exact capabilities. We start very gently — even chair-based exercises — and gradually increase intensity only when the patient is comfortable and ready. We also coordinate with your orthopedic or neuro consultant to ensure everything aligns with their medical advice.
What should the patient wear during sessions?
+
Comfortable, loose-fitting clothes are ideal — cotton salwar kameez, track pants with a T-shirt, or any clothing that allows free movement of arms and legs. Avoid tight jeans or heavy sarees during sessions. For footwear, flat, non-slip shoes or rubber chappals with grip work well. If the session is at home, bare feet on a yoga mat is perfectly fine too.
